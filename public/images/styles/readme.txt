=== TATTOO STYLE SHOWCASE IMAGES ===

Upload images used in the styles catalog carousel directly into this folder.

Naming convention: [style-id]-[image-index].png
Index numbers range from 1 to 4.

Examples:
- black-grey-realism-1.png
- black-grey-realism-2.png
- blackwork-1.png
- neotraditional-1.png

Supported formats: .png, .jpg, .jpeg, .JPG, .PNG
No subfolders are necessary!
